Petra, Jordan Discover the ancient city carved into rose-red sandstone, gradually revealed as you walk through a narrow canyon. Be awed by the Treasury and temples that stand as monuments to past civilizations.
Kyoto, Japan Wander through historic temples and gardens, visit the bamboo grove in Arashiyama, and take part in a traditional tea ceremony. Discover the magic of Japanese culture and history in this blend of modernity and tradition.
Banff, Canada Immerse yourself in the nature of the Canadian Rockies – turquoise lakes, glaciers, and diverse wildlife. Hike breathtaking trails in the summer, or enjoy winter sports with stunning mountain views.
Machu Picchu, Peru Explore the ancient Incan city nestled in the Andes, surrounded by mist and dramatic cliffs. Marvel at the iconic ruins and soak in the mystical atmosphere of one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
